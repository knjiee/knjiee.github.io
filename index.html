About Me

I'm a passionate Web and Software Developer with a focus on building practical systems and web applications that solve real-world problems.

My journey includes developing comprehensive systems during my capstone project, working on real-world solutions during my internship, and creating various management systems and web applications. I have hands-on experience with enterprise-level development, including face recognition systems, loan management, and inventory tracking.

I specialize in backend development using PHP with frameworks like CodeIgniter and Lavalust, and I'm proficient in multiple programming languages including Java, C#, and Visual Basic. My work emphasizes clean code, user-friendly interfaces, and reliable database management.

Whether it's a capstone project, internship assignment, or personal initiative, I approach each project with attention to detail and a commitment to delivering functional, well-documented solutions.
